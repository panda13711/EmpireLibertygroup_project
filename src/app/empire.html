About Empire Liberty Group

Empire Liberty Group transforms aquaculture through modern hatchery science, commercial cage culture and farmer-first partnerships.

Our Mission

Empire Liberty Group specializes in commercial hatcheries, cage culture systems and integrated aquaculture projects across India. Backed by a landmark ₹495 crore MOU with the Government (Nitin Gadkari) and strategic initiatives in Vidarbha, we champion farmer livelihoods, food security and export-quality production.

We combine advanced technologies (biofloc, modern hatchery practices) with farmer outreach and buy-back guarantees to scale sustainable fish production and uplift rural livelihoods.
