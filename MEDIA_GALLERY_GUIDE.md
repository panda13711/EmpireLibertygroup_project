## Common Questions

**Q: Where do I upload the CEO photo?**
A: In "Team Management" section (not Media Gallery). There's a special Founder Photo upload there.

**Q: Can I upload videos?**
A: Currently only images and PDFs are supported. Video support coming soon.

**Q: How do I replace the MOU document?**
A: Just upload a new file in the MOU section - it automatically replaces the old one.

**Q: What happens if I upload too many images?**
A: They're stored in browser localStorage. If it gets full, you'll see an error. Delete old images you don't need.

**Q: Can I see my documents on the main website?**
A: Documents are stored for your records and can be downloaded. They don't automatically appear on the website (for security).

**Q: How do I know which category to choose?**
A: Read the description under each heading - it tells you exactly where those uploads will appear.
